創意寫作提示詞庫 — 12 種實用寫作模板

2026-04-11 閱讀時間：12 分鐘

創意寫作是 Claude 最擅長的應用領域之一。但不同的寫作類型需要不同的提示詞策略。本指南提供 12 種常見創意寫作類型的完整模板，每個都包含具體的風格指引、結構建議和可複製的範例。

1. 短篇故事

短篇故事提示詞模板
Write a short story with the following parameters:

Setting: [時間和地點]
Characters: [主角描述]
Theme: [中心主題]
Length: [字數要求，如 1000-1500 words]
Tone: [語調，如 mysterious, humorous, sad]

Story requirements:
- Include a clear conflict
- Show character development
- Include sensory details (sight, sound, smell)
- End with a meaningful resolution (not necessarily happy)

Writing style:
- Use vivid, specific verbs (not "walked", but "trudged/strolled/dashed")
- Balance dialogue and narrative
- Show, don't tell emotions

Begin the story directly (no preamble).

關鍵技巧：故事需要明確的衝突、角色發展和感官細節。避免籠統的描述。

2. 行銷文案

行銷文案提示詞模板
Write a marketing copy for [產品/服務].

Target audience: [誰會購買]
Key benefit: [最重要的好處]
Product features: [主要特性列表]
Tone: [例如 professional, playful, urgent]
Length: [字數，通常 100-300 for ads]

Copy structure:
1. Hook: Grab attention with a problem or question (1 sentence)
2. Amplify the pain/desire (2-3 sentences)
3. Present solution (your product)
4. Proof/credibility (testimonial, stat, or feature)
5. Call to action (clear, specific)

Style guidelines:
- Use power words (exclusive, limited, proven, revolutionary)
- Address reader with "you" not "customers"
- Include specific benefits, not features
- Create urgency without being pushy

Example CTA: "Get early access for 30% off" (specific, time-bound)

關鍵技巧：好的行銷文案著重於好處（為什麼你需要），而非特性（它有什麼）。

3. 技術文章

技術文章提示詞模板
Write a technical article about [主題].

Target reader: [初學者/中級/進階]
Difficulty level: [難度]
Length: [字數]

Article structure:
1. Introduction (hook + question answered)
2. Background/context (why this matters)
3. Core explanation (step-by-step)
4. Code/practical examples (if applicable)
5. Common pitfalls
6. Performance considerations
7. When to use/not use
8. Further learning

Writing style:
- Assume reader has [specific technical background]
- Explain jargon on first mention
- Use concrete examples over abstractions
- Include visualizations/diagrams hints in [brackets]
- Keep sentences short (< 20 words preferred)

Avoid:
- "Obviously" or "simply"
- Passive voice
- Unnecessary complexity

關鍵技巧：技術寫作需要清晰的結構和漸進式複雜度。包括常見陷阱和何時不使用。

4. 詩歌

詩歌提示詞模板
Write a poem about [主題].

Poetry style: [例如 free verse, haiku, sonnet, modern]
Tone: [例如 melancholic, celebratory, contemplative]
Length: [行數或段落數]
Key themes: [詩歌應涵蓋的主題]

Poetic devices to include:
- Metaphor or simile
- Alliteration or assonance (if appropriate)
- Strong imagery (visual, auditory, tactile)
- Rhythm (if not free verse)

Do NOT:
- Force rhymes that sound awkward
- Use clichés (sunset, moonlight, lost love)
- Over-explain emotions
- Use too many adjectives

Focus on:
- Specific, concrete images
- Emotional authenticity
- Surprise or unexpected connections
- White space and line breaks for impact

關鍵技巧：詩歌應該顯示而非講述。避免陳詞濫調，重視具體意象。

5. 對話寫作

對話寫作提示詞模板
Write dialogue between [人物 A] and [人物 B].

Context: [對話發生的情況]
Conflict/tension: [為什麼對話很重要]
Length: [對話行數或字數]
Period: [時代背景，如現代、1920年]

Dialogue requirements:
- Each character has distinctive voice/speech patterns
- Include subtext (what's unsaid is as important as what's said)
- Use dialogue tags sparingly (said, asked usually enough)
- Show emotion through word choice, not explanation

Character voice examples:
- Character A: [speech pattern - formal? casual? accented?]
- Character B: [speech pattern - different from A]

Common dialogue mistakes to AVOID:
- Using exposition (characters saying things they already know)
- All dialogue sounds the same
- Over-explaining with adverbs ("he said angrily")
- Dialogue that doesn't advance the plot/relationship

After dialogue: [Any action/description that shows consequences]

關鍵技巧：好的對話有潛台詞，角色各有獨特的語音風格。不要讓人物說他們已經知道的事。

6. 人物描寫

人物描寫提示詞模板
Create a detailed character description for [角色名].

Key facts:
- Age: [年齡]
- Occupation: [職業]
- Main goal/desire: [主要目標]
- Main obstacle: [主要障礙]
- Biggest fear: [最大恐懼]

Physical description:
- Build: [體型]
- Distinctive features: [特徵]
- Usual clothing style: [著裝風格]
- Mannerisms: [習慣性動作或姿態]

Personality:
- Strengths: [優點，2-3 個]
- Flaws: [缺點，2-3 個]
- Motivations: [動機]
- Values: [價值觀]
- Quirks: [特別習慣或特異之處]

Backstory:
- Key life event that shaped them: [重要事件]
- Relationship with [other character]: [關係]
- What do they want others to think about them: [表象]
- What's the truth beneath: [真相]

Voice/speech:
- Dialogue patterns: [說話方式]
- Vocabulary level: [詞彙]
- Catchphrases or speech patterns: [習慣用語]

關鍵技巧：好的人物描寫包括內外部特徵、驅動力和內在衝突。避免完美人物。

7. 產品評論

產品評論提示詞模板
Write a product review for [產品名].

Review style: [詳細專家級/簡潔消費者級]
Rating: [5 星級別]
Length: [字數]

Required sections:
1. Quick summary (what is it, price range)
2. What I liked (3-5 specific things)
3. What I didn't like (honest negatives)
4. Performance/durability (if applicable)
5. Who should buy this
6. Who shouldn't
7. Final verdict with rating

Writing requirements:
- Be specific (not "great quality" but "no fraying after 6 months of daily use")
- Include both positives and negatives (no perfect reviews)
- Use comparisons to competitors if relevant
- Consider value-for-money
- Provide context (how long have I used it)

Tone: [Honest, helpful, slightly opinionated but fair]

Do NOT:
- Be clickbait negative
- Inflate positives
- Assume reader needs
- Include sponsored content disclosure here

關鍵技巧：最有幫助的評論是具體的、平衡的，並承認不同用戶的不同需求。

8. 社群媒體文案

社群媒體文案提示詞模板
Write a [Instagram/Twitter/LinkedIn/TikTok] post.

Platform: [特定平台]
Goal: [推廣/教育/娛樂/互動]
Target audience: [誰]
Key message: [主要訊息]
Tone: [風格]

Platform-specific requirements:

INSTAGRAM:
- Hook in first 1-2 lines (captures before "more")
- Include relevant hashtags (10-30)
- Call to action (like/comment/DM/link in bio)
- Caption can be 2-3 paragraphs

TWITTER/X:
- Under 280 characters (or 1-2 tweet thread)
- Hook immediately
- Include 1-2 relevant hashtags
- Use conversational language

LINKEDIN:
- Professional but personable
- Industry insight or career tip
- Invite discussion
- Use paragraph breaks for readability

TIKTOK:
- Hook in first 3 seconds (video description)
- Engaging, casual tone
- Trending sounds/music reference (if applicable)
- Call to action

General best practices:
- Include visual description [image/video describes this]
- Emoji use (platform dependent)
- Timing if relevant ("New drop Friday!")
- Authentic voice over corporate speak

關鍵技巧：每個平台有不同的文化和格式。優化你的 hook 在用戶滾動過程中捕捉注意力。

9. 電郵副本

電郵副本提示詞模板
Write a [newsletters/promotional/onboarding/re-engagement] email.

Email type: [類型]
Length: [字數]
Primary goal: [主要目標]
Target reader: [誰]
Send timing: [何時發送]

Email structure:
1. Subject line (short, benefit-driven)
2. Preheader (complements subject)
3. Greeting (personalized if possible)
4. Hook (why this matters to reader)
5. Body (main value delivery)
6. CTA (clear, single primary action)
7. Signature (name, company, socials)

Subject line tips:
- Create curiosity without clickbait
- Personalization (if possible)
- Numbers or specificity
- Keep under 50 characters

Body best practices:
- Short paragraphs (2-3 lines)
- Use scannability (subheadings, bullets)
- Conversational tone
- Focus on reader benefit
- One clear CTA (not multiple)

CTA button text:
- Specific: "Get Early Access" not "Click Here"
- Action-oriented verb
- Sense of urgency or benefit

Do NOT:
- Excessive graphics (slow loading)
- Too many fonts or colors
- Assume reader has context
- Make CTA hard to find

關鍵技巧：好的電郵聚焦於讀者的收益，而不是推銷。清晰的單一 CTA 比多個選項更有效。

10. 故事綱要（Outline）

故事綱要提示詞模板
Create a detailed story outline for [故事概念].

Genre: [類型]
Target length: [字數或頁數]
Target audience: [讀者]

Three-act structure:
ACT 1: Setup (inciting incident, character introduction)
- Scene 1: [描述]
- Scene 2: [描述]
- Inciting incident: [改變一切的事件]

ACT 2: Confrontation (raising stakes, obstacles)
- Scene 3: [描述]
- Complication: [事情變糟]
- Midpoint turning point: [高點或低點]
- Scene 4-5: [更多衝突]

ACT 3: Resolution (climax, falling action, resolution)
- Climactic scene: [最大衝突]
- Consequences: [結果]
- Resolution: [如何解決]
- Epilogue (optional): [故事後來]

Character arcs:
- [Character]: starts as [X], ends as [Y]
- [Character]: main conflict/growth

Themes to weave in:
- [主題 1]
- [主題 2]

Key scenes (must include):
- [必要場景 1]
- [必要場景 2]

Pacing notes:
- Estimate page count per section
- Where to place reveals/twists
- Where to plant foreshadowing

關鍵技巧：固體的綱要節省寫作時間。包括角色弧、主題和關鍵場景。

11. 旅遊指南文章

旅遊指南提示詞模板
Write a travel guide article for [地點].

Perspective: [本地人/首次訪客/預算旅行者]
Season: [季節或全年]
Length: [字數]
Style: [冒險/實用/奢侈/預算]

Must-include sections:
1. Quick facts (best time to visit, visa, language)
2. Getting there (transportation options)
3. Where to stay (neighborhood recommendations + specific hotels)
4. Food & dining (signature dishes, restaurants)
5. Must-see attractions (with why, not just what)
6. Hidden gems (less touristy spots)
7. Day trip options
8. Practical tips (safety, money, customs)
9. Local experiences (authentic activities)
10. Budget breakdown (estimated costs)

Writing style:
- Use second person ("you'll want to...")
- Include sensory descriptions (smells, sounds, tastes)
- Personal anecdotes or perspective
- Specific recommendations (not generic)
- Honest about crowds, costs, etc.

Include:
- Opening scene that captures the place's essence
- Realistic time allocations (how long things take)
- Cost ranges
- Local phrases in native language

關鍵技巧：最有幫助的旅遊指南包括實踐建議、當地視角和誠實的缺點。

12. 個人隨筆

個人隨筆提示詞模板
Write a personal essay about [主題].

Essay type: [回憶錄/觀點/反思/教訓]
Length: [字數]
Tone: [誠實/懷舊/感悟/熱情]
Audience: [誰應該關心這個]

Essay structure:
1. Opening (vivid scene or question that hooks)
2. Context (why this matters, background)
3. The conflict/tension (what happened, what I thought, what changed)
4. Reflection (what I learned, how I changed)
5. Broader implications (why reader should care)
6. Closing (not a conclusion, but an ending moment)

Writing tips:
- Use specific details (not "I was nervous" but "my hands shook")
- Include dialogue (makes it alive)
- Don't explain the lesson, let reader discover it
- Be vulnerable (honesty resonates)
- Avoid sermonizing

Narrative arc:
- Start with a moment/memory
- Show the growth/change
- End with a new understanding

Tone requirements:
- Conversational but thoughtful
- Personal without being self-indulgent
- Honest about failings
- Not preachy

Avoid:
- Telling reader how to feel
- Focusing only on negative/victim narrative
- Generic universal truths
- Patronizing tone

關鍵技巧：強有力的隨筆顯示變化，而不是解釋。讀者應該發現教訓，而不是被告知。

實用提示：混合和匹配

這些模板可以組合使用：

  • 故事 + 對話： 使用對話模板來改進故事中的交談
  • 人物描寫 + 故事： 先建立角色，然後寫故事
  • 社群媒體 + 產品評論： 為 Instagram 撰寫簡短評論
  • 電郵 + 行銷文案： 使用文案技巧編寫電郵正文

核心要點

  • ✅ 不同寫作類型需要不同的結構和聲音
  • ✅ 清晰的指引提升輸出質量 40%+
  • ✅ 包括「避免什麼」與「做什麼」一樣重要
  • ✅ 詳細的人物設定和背景提供更好的故事
  • ✅ 具體細節比籠統描述更有力
  • ✅ 聲音和語調是區分不同寫作類型的關鍵
