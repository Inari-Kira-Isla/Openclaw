## 摘要

Transform your OpenClaw/Nanobot from AI assistant into a money-earning AI coworker.
- $10K in 7 Hours
- 44+ 產業
- 從助理 → 同事的進化

---

## 詳細內容

### 一、核心願景

| 項目 | 內容 |
|------|------|
| 標語 | AI doesn't just assist — it works as your true coworker |
| 目標 | $10K+ in 7 hours |
| 範圍 | 44+ 產業 |

### 二、覆蓋產業

| 類別 | 產業 |
|------|------|
| Technology & Engineering | Developer, Mechanical Engineer, Industrial Engineer |
| Business & Finance | Accountant, Financial Manager, Investment Analyst |
| Healthcare & Social Services | Nurse Practitioner, Registered Nurse, Pharmacist |
| Legal, Media & Operations | Lawyer, Journalist, Producer, Ops Manager |

### 三、Key Features

1. **AI Assistant → AI Coworker Evolution**
   - 從幫手變成真正的同事
   - 完成真實工作，創造經濟價值

2. **Live Economic Benchmark**
   - GDPVal 數據集 (220 tasks)
   - AI 必須賺取收入、支付 Token 費用、維持經濟償付能力

3. **Production AI Validation**
   - 衡量指標：工作品質、成本效率、長期生存
   - 不只是技術基準

4. **Multi-Model Competition Arena**
   - 支援模型：GLM, Kimi, Qwen
   - 通過實際工作表現競爭

### 四、技術規格

| 項目 | 規格 |
|------|------|
| Language | Python ≥ 3.10 |
| License | MIT |
| Dataset | GDPVal (220 tasks) |
| Benchmark | Economic survival |
| Feature | Nanobot integration |
